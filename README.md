Key Activities:

A hotel management system relies on key activities that can be efficiently managed through SQL queries.
Room management tracks room inventory, including updates on availability.
Customers management involves adding and retrieving guest details for personalized service.
Reservations management handles the creation and modification of reservations, while payment processing records transactions and retrieves payment histories. 
payment processing handles the recording of payments and the retrieval of payment histories linked to specific bookings.
